Why I want to become a developer?

I want to become a software developer because I am passionate about technology and problem-solving. Development allows me to build real-world solutions, create useful applications, and continuously learn new skills. I enjoy writing code, understanding how systems work, and improving efficiency through logic and creativity. Being a developer also gives me the opportunity to work on innovative projects and contribute to the digital world. It provides strong career growth, flexibility, and the ability to work in different domains across industries.
